Meet Parab Sir

For fifteen years, Parab Sir has taught Accountancy and Economics the slow, honest way — building each idea from the ground up until it stops feeling like a subject and starts feeling like common sense.

A former Chartered Accountant, he left a busy practice because he missed the part he loved most: explaining. He started these classes from a single rented room with nine students, and the rule has never changed — keep batches small, learn every name, and stay until the child truly understands.

Today the room is fuller, but the promise is the same. He still marks every test by hand and still picks up the phone himself when a parent calls.
