Questions

Frequently asked

Do you teach both HSC and CBSE boards?

Yes. Class XII runs as separate HSC and CBSE batches so that notes, paper patterns and revision schedules match each board precisely. Class XI is taught together with board-specific guidance where the syllabi differ.

How large are the batches?

Batches are deliberately capped — 24 for Class XI, smaller for board batches, and 30 for CA Foundation — so faculty can track every student and run meaningful doubt-solving sessions.

Are weekly tests compulsory?

Yes. Every batch sits a weekly test under exam conditions, and a rank list is published. This is central to our method and is included in the fee.

Can I attend a class before joining?

Absolutely. We offer a free demo class for every programme. Book one from the Contact page and sit through a regular session before deciding.

Is the fee payable in instalments?

The listed annual fees can typically be paid in two or three instalments across the year. Please confirm the current schedule with the office — details are placeholders on this demo site.
