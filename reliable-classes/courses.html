FAQ

Common questions

How are the weekly tests ranked?

Every weekly test is graded on the same scale across the batch and a class rank is published. Parents and students can see the score, the rank and the per-chapter accuracy after each test.

What does "doubt tracking" actually mean?

Each doubt raised in a 1:1 session is logged against the student. Faculty follow up in the next session until the underlying concept is cleared — it is not closed just because the session ended.

Can fees be paid in instalments?

Yes. All fees shown are placeholders and can be split into instalments. Please confirm the current schedule and any sibling or early-enrolment concessions at enquiry.

When do batches start?

XI and XII batches begin in June, aligned to the academic year. CA Foundation batches run twice a year ahead of the ICAI exam windows. Contact us for the next available batch.

Is there a diagnostic test before joining?

Yes — every student takes a diagnostic test in the first week. It sets the baseline against which all later progress is measured. It does not affect admission.
