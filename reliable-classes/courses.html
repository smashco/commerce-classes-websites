XI Commerce — Foundation year

Maharashtra State Board (HSC) · 11 months · batch capped at 24

₹38,000Full session

Subjects & units covered

  • 01Book-Keeping & Accountancy — journal, ledger, trial balance, bills of exchange
  • 02Organisation of Commerce & Management — business services, entrepreneurship
  • 03Economics — micro fundamentals, demand & supply, national income
  • 04Secretarial Practice — company formation, sources of finance
  • 05Mathematics & Statistics — ratios, functions, measures of dispersion
  • 06English — comprehension, applied writing, prescribed text

Test schedule & what we track

  • One full Saturday paper each week, rotating across the six subjects
  • Two term exams on the full board pattern (October, February)
  • Rank sheet posted every Monday; trend kept per student all year
  • Tracked: unit completion, weekly rank, attendance, open doubts

Installments: ₹15,000 at admission, then two payments of ₹11,500 in July and November. No charge for the Week-1 diagnostic.

XII Commerce — Board year

Maharashtra State Board (HSC) · 10 months · batch capped at 22

₹44,000Full session

Subjects & units covered

  • 01Book-Keeping & Accountancy — partnership, company accounts, analysis of statements
  • 02Organisation of Commerce & Management — principles, functions, marketing
  • 03Economics — macro, money & banking, public finance, index numbers
  • 04Secretarial Practice — capital, deposits, dividends, correspondence
  • 05Mathematics & Statistics — LPP, commission, annuities, probability
  • 06English — section-wise board answer technique & prescribed text

Test schedule & what we track

  • Weekly Saturday paper, plus a full board-pattern mock once a month
  • Two prelims in January under exam conditions and timing
  • Accountancy and Maths get a second mid-week problem drill
  • Tracked: per-subject mock scores, time-per-section, doubt log to March

Installments: ₹18,000 at admission, then two payments of ₹13,000 in July and October. Prelim answer-script review is included.

CA Foundation

ICAI Foundation course · 8 months · batch capped at 20

₹52,000Full session

Papers & units covered

  • 01Paper 1 — Accounting: depreciation, inventory, partnership, company accounts
  • 02Paper 2 — Business Laws: Indian Contract Act, Sale of Goods, Companies Act basics
  • 03Paper 3 — Quantitative Aptitude: business maths, logical reasoning, statistics
  • 04Paper 4 — Business Economics: demand & supply, market structures, finance

Test schedule & what we track

  • Two timed tests every week — one subjective, one objective (MCQ)
  • Negative-marking discipline drilled for Papers 3 & 4 from week one
  • Two full ICAI-pattern mock attempts before the exam window
  • Tracked: per-paper accuracy, MCQ speed, attempt strategy, doubt log

Installments: ₹20,000 at admission, then two payments of ₹16,000 spaced eight weeks apart. ICAI registration is handled by the student; we assist with the timeline.

Questions

Before you enrol

The questions families ask most often at the admission desk.

Is the Week-1 diagnostic test compulsory, and does it cost anything?

It is free and we ask every student to sit it before admission is confirmed. It is how we place you in the right pace group; enrolling without it would mean teaching you blind.

What happens if I fall behind on the unit deadlines?

The wall chart and monthly report flag it early. Students more than one unit behind are given a scheduled catch-up slot and the relevant doubts are logged until cleared — it is built into the system, not an extra charge.

Can I see the rank sheet, or is it private?

The weekly rank sheet is posted by name for the batch. We have found that visible, fair ranking pushes the whole group; your detailed report, however, goes only to you and your parents.

Are the fee installments fixed, or can timing be adjusted?

The amounts are fixed but installment dates can be shifted by a few weeks on request at the time of admission. Speak to the office; there is no interest or surcharge on the standard schedule.

Do you coach XI and CA Foundation together for a student?

Yes, several students run XII Commerce alongside CA Foundation. The two timetables are set so test Saturdays do not clash, and the combined load is reviewed in the monthly report.
