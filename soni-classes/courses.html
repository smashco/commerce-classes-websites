Frequently asked

Questions parents and students ask

Q.Which boards do you coach for?

We coach the Maharashtra State Board (HSC) and CBSE Commerce streams. Most of our XI and XII students are HSC; CBSE students are taught the same chapters against the CBSE pattern and sit the CBSE-pattern Saturday tests.

Q.How large is a batch?

Every batch is capped at 24 students and we do not exceed it. The cap is the point of the place — it is what lets a teacher check Accountancy work by name rather than collect it in a pile.

Q.Can my child attend a demonstration lecture first?

Yes. We offer one free demonstration lecture in Accountancy before you decide, by appointment. Write to us through the enquiry form or call the office, and we will place your child in the next suitable batch lecture.

Q.Can the fee be paid in instalments?

Yes. Fees may be paid in two instalments — the first at admission and the second before the start of the second term, typically October. Please discuss the schedule with the office at the time of admission.

Q.Are notes and tests charged separately?

No. Chapter-wise printed notes and the full Saturday test series are included in the fee shown for each programme. There is no separate material, registration or examination charge.

Q.What if my child joins after the term has begun?

A mid-term joiner sits the diagnostic test, receives the printed notes for chapters already covered, and is given two extra doubt sessions to catch up before the next Saturday test. Seats permitting, we accept joiners up to the first prelim.

Ready to begin? Send an enquiry and we will arrange a demonstration lecture and a seat in the right batch.
